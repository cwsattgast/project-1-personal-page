Hello, I’m Charlie.

I live in Portland, Oregon

Professional Life

My professional life shares three areas of focus: serving as web and production manager at DigitalScrapper.com, Civil Air Patrol chaplaincy, and assisting in ministry at The Oregon Community.

Business: I am a partner with my wife in DigitalScrapper.com, a web-based company that provides Photoshop and Photoshop Elements training for the scrapbooking community. I serve the company as a very flexible generalist with a background in printing, publishing, web, video, audio, company finance, CD and DVD replication, and the ability to troubleshoot just about anything.

Ministry: I have served in various expressions of pastoral ministry since 1994. My skills include preaching, teaching, disciplemaking, music, and pastoral counseling. In my role as a chaplain I serve as Deputy Chief of the Civil Air Patrol Chaplain Corps, have ongoing involvement with Civil Air Patrol cadets at the local squadron level, and maintain currency in emergency services (search and rescue, homeland security, and disaster response).
